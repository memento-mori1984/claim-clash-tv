HOW TO TEST (PLEASE EXPLORE)
----------------------------
Menus and wording
  - Read labels on the welcome screen, toolbar, Settings, About, and Rules.
  - Note any text that is confusing, too small, or easy to misread.

Core gameplay
  - Try Team A / Team B turns, Ask, and Follow Up.
  - Try Single Player Interrogation Mode and Start New Session.
  - Use Load Example for a sample question.
  - Try Bookmark Concern and Manage bookmarks.

AI features
  - Confirm your primary AI answers in the main box.
  - Toggle other AIs and use Query Selected to compare responses.
  - Note any provider errors or answers that are hard to read.

Cast to TV (important)
  - On the same Wi-Fi, click Cast to TV on the PC.
  - Open the shown URL in a TV or phone web browser.
  - Ask a question on the PC and confirm the mirror updates on the cast screen.
  - Report connection problems, layout issues, or stale content.

Feedback (please use the app)
  - Prefer the built-in feedback form instead of writing a separate email from scratch.
  - Use either path: Send Alpha Tester Feedback on the welcome screen, or the Feedback button on the main screen (same form).
  - Fill in the form, then choose your email provider to send. The To field and subject are pre-filled.
  - Send feedback when you finish testing or whenever something breaks.
  - Say what you tried, what worked, and what broke.
