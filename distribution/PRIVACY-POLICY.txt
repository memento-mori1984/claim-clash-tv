SUMMARY
-------
- No developer servers receive your API keys, conversations, or game content.
- API keys and settings stay on your device (localStorage).
- AI requests go directly from your device to the provider you configure.
- Session exports save locally unless you email or upload them yourself.
- No developer analytics, ads, or crash telemetry.

STORE LISTING URL
-----------------
Public privacy policy URL (Microsoft Store):
  https://claim-clash.com/download/privacy-policy

Enter that URL in Microsoft Partner Center. Update privacyPolicyUrl in version.json
to match before building a Store release.

DATA ON YOUR DEVICE
-------------------
- API keys and preferences (localStorage)
- Brain question pool / daily question (localStorage)
- Exported sessions (Documents\Claim Clash\)

NETWORK USE
-----------
- AI providers: your questions/context sent with YOUR API key to THAT provider
- Brain: may fetch creator-signed daily question feed (HTTPS; verified in app)
- Cast to TV: local LAN HTTP server with pairing token
- Feedback: only if YOU send email via your mail provider

WE DO NOT COLLECT
-----------------
Accounts, automatic uploads of keys/conversations, telemetry, or ad tracking.
