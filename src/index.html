Core Rules
• Red or Blue starts by typing a clear, specific question in the CURRENT QUESTION box and pressing Enter (or clicking the Enter button). Gemini then provides an evidence-based response.
• Players alternate turns. After reviewing the answer, click Follow Up to switch to the other player.
• Questions should seek evidence, clarification, or verification of specific claims.
• Keep questions relevant to the ongoing thread for coherent exploration.
• Maintain respect and focus on the claims themselves, not on each other.
