Core Rules
• One focused question per turn.
• Questions should seek evidence, clarification, or verification of specific claims.
• Keep questions relevant to the current topic.
• Follow-up questions are encouraged when more detail or counter-evidence is needed.
• Maintain respect and focus on the claims, not on each other.
• Ask the AI to ground its responses in primary sources and documented facts.
