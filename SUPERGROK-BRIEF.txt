=== WHAT IT IS ===
Claim Clash is a Windows desktop app (Tauri v2 + Rust backend + HTML/JS frontend) for a two-player conversation game. Players alternate turns asking evidence-based questions to a shared AI about polarizing topics. Goal: shared understanding, not debate wins. Solo mode available. Optional Cast-to-TV on LAN. Compare multiple AI providers side by side.

Tagline: "Built for two" — evidence-based questions with a shared AI.
